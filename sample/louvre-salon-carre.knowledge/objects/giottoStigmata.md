# giottoStigmata

## Title

Saint Francis of Assisi Receiving the Stigmata

## Artist

Giotto di Bondone

## Identification

- date: ca. 1300-1325
- medium: gold ground on poplar panel
- dimensions: about 3.13 x 1.63 m
- Louvre location: Salle 708, Aile Denon, Niveau 1

## What Is Shown

- Saint Francis receiving the stigmata
- a predella below with scenes including the dream of Innocent III, papal approval of the order and Francis preaching to the birds
- an inscription on the frame naming Giotto as Florentine

## Talking Points

- fruehes 14. Jahrhundert
- deutlich greifbarere Landschaft und Koerperlichkeit
- wichtig fuer Erzaehlbarkeit und Raumsinn
- Franziskus erscheint nicht nur heilig, sondern auch praesent in einer Welt

## What To Notice

- the event feels placed in a setting, not only displayed as a holy sign
- Francis has weight and posture; he occupies ground rather than floating before it
- the predella expands the work into a narrative sequence
- even with gold still present, the painting pulls the viewer toward lived experience and story

## Why It Matters

- this is a strong museum example for explaining why Giotto is often treated as a turning point
- the picture preserves sacred gravity while making bodies, landscape and event more graspable
- the predella helps a guide speak about early narrative altarpieces rather than a single isolated image

## Comparison Hooks

- compared with Cimabue, Giotto brings more believable place, gravity and event
- compared with Fra Angelico, Giotto can feel less ethereal and more anchored in earthly reality
- compared with Uccello, Giotto uses structure in the service of narrative presence rather than geometric display

## Provenance Notes

- associated with San Francesco in Pisa
- later moved through San Nicola and the Camposanto in Pisa
- taken to the Louvre in 1813

## Guide Notes

Hier kann ein Guide gut zeigen, warum Giotto oft als Wendepunkt empfunden wird:

- weniger ikonische Distanz
- mehr anschauliche Situation
- mehr Gewicht, Geste und Ort

Gute Anschlussfragen:

- Wo spueren wir hier zum ersten Mal wirklich "Ort"?
- Was erzaehlt die Predella zusaetzlich?
- Warum wirkt die Heiligkeit hier naeher und weniger unnahbar?
